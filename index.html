User guide

Control using keyboad

  • [ENTER] - Start/stop
  • [SPACE] - Sequence
  • [f] - Fill
  • [LEFT arrow] - Prev instrument left
  • [RIGHT arrow] - Next instrument
  • [UP arrow] - Volume up
  • [DOWN arrow] - Volume down
  • [C] - Clear
  • [Ctrl C] - Clear sequence
  • [1] - Sequence 1
  • [2] - Sequence 2
  • [3] - Sequence 3
  • [4] - Sequence 4
